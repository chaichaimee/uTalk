Beyond Standard Feedback

uTalk isn’t just a notification tool—it’s a bridge between your workflow and your creativity. Stop settling for generic system sounds. With uTalk, every command becomes an experience tailored to your personal preference, giving life to your clipboard and file operations like never before.

Whether you're working in a complex code editor or managing a vast directory of files, uTalk provides the auditory confirmation you need, in the language and style you choose.


Hot Keys

NVDA+Alt+T

Single Tap : Instant Language Toggle

Double Tap : Open uTalk Setting

Effortlessly switch between your configured user languages (e.g., English, Thai, etc.) on the fly. Adapt your environment to your current task with a single keystroke.


Premium Capabilities

1. Total Command Awareness

Receive immediate, customizable spoken feedback for essential actions: Copy, Paste, Cut, Save, Select All, and Undo. Never second-guess if your command registered again.

2. Advanced Context Monitoring

From text editors and word processors to the depths of Windows Explorer, uTalk follows you everywhere, ensuring consistent feedback across your entire digital workspace.

3. Professional Customization Panel

Take full control of your experience. The dedicated settings panel allows you to redefine spoken messages for every major operation including Copy as Path, File Operations, Redo, and more.

4. Intelligent Persistence

Your configuration is your own. uTalk utilizes a robust JSON-based architecture to save your preferences automatically, ensuring your custom "imagination-driven" setup is ready the moment you start.


Getting Started

Experience the future of auditory feedback in three simple steps:

  1. Integrate the add-on into your NVDA environment.
  2. Launch the settings to craft your custom voice responses.
  3. Enjoy a truly personalized, high-efficiency workflow.
